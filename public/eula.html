2. How the Software Works

WayMark is a browser-based application. Nearly all data processing — including reading your Google Sheets and Drive files, analyzing and importing data, and rendering templates — runs in your web browser. The WayMark server serves the application code, handles OAuth token exchange, and provides a URL-fetch proxy for recipe imports. The server does not access, process, or store your Google files or data.

By using the Software you acknowledge that: your browser communicates directly with Google APIs to read and write your data; imported content is processed in your browser and written to your Google Drive; when importing recipes from URLs, the page HTML is fetched through the server (as a CORS proxy) and returned to your browser for client-side parsing; user preferences are stored in your Google Drive and/or browser local storage; and the WayMark server has no access to your file contents.
