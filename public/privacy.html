Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Overview

WayMark helps users interact with data stored in Google Sheets and Google Drive. This policy explains what information is processed and how it is used.

2. Information We Process

  • OAuth tokens required to access approved Google APIs
  • Google Drive and Google Sheets metadata/content needed for app features

3. Google API Data Use

4. Session Handling

OAuth is handled through Google. Access tokens are short-lived and used for authorized requests. Refresh tokens may be stored in secure, HTTP-only cookies on the server.

5. Data Sharing

WayMark does not sell personal information. Data is shared only with authorized Google services, infrastructure providers required to run the app, or where required by law.

6. Security

WayMark applies reasonable safeguards such as HTTPS in production and secure cookie settings. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Choices

You can revoke access anytime in your Google Account permissions settings and stop using the app at any time.
